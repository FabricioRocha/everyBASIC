This is officially the last version launched by Commodore of its famous 8-bit BASIC, and what an improvement it was. Sprites, input devices, more graphics and text-mode commands. Unfortunately, Commodore didn't bother backporting it to its most popular and long-lived machine.

===== Where it is/was used =====
  * Commodore 128 (USA, 1985)
