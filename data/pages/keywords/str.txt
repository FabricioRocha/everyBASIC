====== STR$ ======
Returns a string representation of a numerical value.

**Implemented by:** [[:basics:bwbasic:bw32]], [[:basics:FreeBASIC:]]

**With variations:** [[:basics:ugBASIC:]] (see [[STR-S|STR$]])
